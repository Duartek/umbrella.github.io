About Us

Founded 36 years ago by Tom Poulin CGR, Umbrella Designs has become a fixture in New Mexico.
From the very beginning basic principals of customer satisfaction have guided Tom and all those who have worked with him. Umbrella Designs concept was conceived by Tom after listening to his customers concerns, “I don’t want to be torn up for months”, “I want to trust the workers in my home”, and “I don’t want to live with the mess”, we went to work and created a unique system of design, product selection, and Installation- all with our customer’s satisfaction in mind.
Umbrella Designs – with its Experienced Designers, Interactive Design Experience, Unique product Selection, Seasoned Builders & Quality Work is the new and smart way to remodel your home!
